Things not to miss in Los Angeles

Venture to the Griffith Observatory

Travel to the farthest reaches of the universe in the Samuel Oschin Planetarium. Live presentations immerse you in the wonder and meaning of the cosmos.

Dive into Venice Beach

Enjoy the amazing ocean views, sunbathe, or bike ride along the boardwalk. Go play in the sand and surf the waves as you catch a beautiful Venice beach sunset.

Take a ride to Universal Studios

Take the world-famous studio tour, constructed with creative advice from Stephen Spielberg. Peek at 13 blocks of back-lot movie and television show sets, plus some simulated thrills.
